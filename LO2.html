We started by interviewing Studio Krom to understand their goals and expectations. From there, we dove into research on dyslexia, especially how it affects reading and comprehension. In this phase, i created personas based on the audience we were designing for. Once we had a clear direction, we started experimenting with concepts. We gathered feedback, made changes, and kept adjusting until the effects felt both impactful and understandable.

Deliver

We took the most effective concepts and turned them into a working prototype. We ran quick user tests to see how people responded. Did they understand the message, and did the experience actually make them reflect on what dyslexia feels like?
